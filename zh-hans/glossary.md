## Special Patterns

- `:icon:`: Keep the icon as it.
- `{{parameter}}`: Keep the parameter as it, translate surrounding text.
- `^{{text}}^<{{tooltip}}>`: Keep sentence structure, translate text and tooltip.
- `\n`: New line, keep.
- Markdown (`**`, `*`, `#`): Keep syntax.
